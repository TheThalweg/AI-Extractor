Europe Weekly Kickstart: What to expect from the Q1 2026 earnings season
10 April 2026 | 1:52PM BST | Research | Portfolio Strategy

●

The European earnings season kicks off next week. By the end of May, roughly 70% of the STOXX 600's market cap will have reported.

●

Consensus expects earnings to increase by 4% in Q1 2026, with the bulk of the growth coming from commodity-related sectors.

●

Commodity-related companies are protecting their margins amid the energy cost shock, pushing aggregate margins for the market upwards but eroding them for the market ex-commodities.

●

We expect consensus earnings growth of 13% for 2026 to come down to closer to our 5% estimate, as we move into the season. At the same time, experience from the 2022 energy shock points to upside risks to our below-consensus forecast.
