China Weekly Kickstart: Markets gained 3-4%; Regulators urged for more disciplined competitions; PPI turned positive
11 April 2026 | 3:56AM HKT | Research | Portfolio Strategy

MXCN/CSI300 gained 3%/4% this week. On April 9, the SAMR, NDRC and CAC jointly convened a guidance meeting to implement the "Rules on Pricing Behavior of Internet Platforms", requiring the Internet platforms to effectively regulate subsidy practices and curb predatory price competition. Simultaneously, four government departments held a symposium for enterprises in the power and energy storage battery industry, calling for the continuous and in-depth advancement of capacity warning systems and the standardization of price competition. The Ministry of Culture and Tourism estimated that the total number of domestic trips during the three-day Qingming Festival holiday reached 135mn (+6.8% yoy), while total domestic tourism expenditure amounted to 614bn yuan (+6.6% yoy). PPI inflation turned positive (+0.5% yoy) in March for the first time since late 2022, while headline CPI inflation moderated to +1.0% yoy from +1.3% yoy in February. 67%/95% of the China/MSCI China universe have reported so far with CY25 earnings rising 11%/declining 5% yoy.
