The Core PCE-PCI Gap: We examined the unusually large positive gap between core PCE and CPI y/y inflation, which widened to about +60bp in January 2026, reversing the historical norm of negative 40bps. We believe that recent strength in core PCE reflects reinforcing composition effects, rather than broader inflation momentum. Weight differences have amplified the pass​-​through of tariff​-​sensitive goods inflation into PCE while muting the effect of used‑car deflation, and stronger health care services inflation has mattered more, given its higher PCE weight.
