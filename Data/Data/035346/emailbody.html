IQVIA weekly script data, Oral Wegovy achieves ~105.4k TRx in Week 13 of launch, +3.9% WoW, strong early uptake vs. historical analogues, even missing non​-​Retail, as NOVOB comments suggest only c.60% capture. Note that wow performance will be impacted by Good Friday holiday in the US. Oral Wegovy week 13W TRx of 105,366 compares to week 13 TRx of 13,057 and 58,692 for NOVOB's Injectable Wegovy and LLY's Injectable Zepbound, respectively, i.e. the early Oral Wegovy had an initial strong launch, albeit in a now far better established Obesity market, vs. building the Obesity market. Notably, LLY’s (covered by Emily Field) Foundayo (orforglipron) was made available in the US via LillyDirect and telehealth providers and is shipping to retail pharmacies nationwide beginning Thursday, April 9th. ...
