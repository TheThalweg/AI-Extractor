Charts Worth a Look: Implications From Muse Spark

Against much skepticism from the press and investment community, Meta reasserted itself into the conversation among frontier labs with the release of Muse Spark this week. While not yet at the frontier, the scaffolding is in place for Meta to make another historic run, this time in AI.

10 April 2026  |  5 MIN READ

Read the Report

The Key Take​-​Away: The Muse Spark release was enough for us to conclude that META is back in the AI race. Many investors we spoke to over the past few months were skeptical that this was possible given attempts by MSFT, AMZN, and others. While Spark is not yet at the frontier, in just nine months Meta Superintelligence Lab (MSL) has rebuilt the pretraining and RL stack, which should make for quick model deployment going forward. We'd note that it took Grok and most of the Chinese AI labs several attempts before achieving the near​-​frontier status that MSL hit on its first at-bat. The MSL team seems highly capable and engaged, in contrast to recent press.
