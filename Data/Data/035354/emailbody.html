Cross Atlantic Weekly Hybrid Monitor

We update the latest performance and valuations metrics for European and US hybrid capital (AT1, Bank Preferred, LT2, Sub Inco, Corporate Hybrids).

10 April 2026  |  4 MIN READ

Read the Report

Three main takes

1. In Europe, hybrids tightened materially this week following the de​-​escalation in the Middle East conflict. AT1s led the hybrids move, delivering the largest percentage tightening and now screen at the 23rd percentile of their 12​-​month range, appearing rich relative to bank T2s and corporate hybrids, which sit closer to the 50th percentile.

2. Derivatives outperformed cash this week with the Xover versus corporate hybrids ratio declining sharply over the week and now sitting marginally inside pre​-​conflict levels. Consistent with improved risk sentiment, the corporate hybrids–senior basis also compressed materially (-16bp WoW), and also screens tighter than pre​-​conflict levels.
