COMMODITY FACT SHEET

ALUMINIUM

 

 

The focus of the aluminium market has shifted to the Middle East. After escaping unscathed during the first weeks of the war, two smelters were hit by a drone attack and suffered significant damage. Reflecting the risk of significant supply disruptions, prices and physical premiums have risen as a result. 


While part of the Middle East supply disruption will be offset by increasing production elsewhere, it will also require demand destruction. We thus lifted our three-month price target but left our twelve-month target unchanged, expecting a normalisation of market conditions. Volatility is set to stay elevated.
