CIO WEEKLY

The Swiss hedgehog’s spines are selective
 
 

Dear Reader,


The underperformance of Swiss equities during the latest geopolitical turmoil has raised questions about their status as safe-haven assets. The short‑term behaviour of Swiss equities is best explained by index concentration and idiosyncratic stock effects. We continue to view the CHF and Swiss assets more broadly as offering investors an additional source of risk premium and enhanced portfolio diversification.


Find out more about our views and the topics discussed in the latest CIO Weekly:

  • A ceasefire is welcome but will not last forever
  • Are Swiss equities a safe-haven asset?
  • Swiss equities are not a hedge against an inflation-driven shock
