Aggregate Scripts (TRx) for current generation GLP1 class - Mounjaro, Zepbound, Ozempic, and Wegovy - were flat for the week ending 4/3/2026 (vs +4% w/w previously) and now flat on a trailing 4-week (T4W) basis. Oral Wegovy closed its 3rd month on market above other launches and kicked off April with ~100K TRx (+4% w/w). That said, oral Weg's NBRx remains flat and we think will be pressed by Foundayo's launch. LLY still dominates the Diabesity TRx landscape with ~60% share vs NVO's 40%. Some other key findings that stuck out from this week's IQVIA update:

  • NBRx for Zep pen and vial going in opposite direction, we think KwikPen's strong entry is the likely culprit (see Fig 7). For 4/3/2026, Zepbound Kwikpen TRx reached ~30K (+5% w/w or +29% on T4W basis). Zep and MJO's CBRx are steadily creeping up. (see Fig 8) However, given the NBRx changes from Zep Kwikpen, we're expecting Zep's vial CBRx to start moderating.
  • Oral Wegovy's NBRx / starter dose trend is looking flat. TRx for oral Weg's 4 mg dose was at ~37K for 4/3/2026 vs ~49K TRx for oral Wegovy's 1.5 mg starter dose from a month prior, implying ~75% of NBRx patients titrating upwards or perhaps ~25% discontinuation.
  • Whether Foundayo's launch starts blunting Wegovy and Zep's CBRx trends is on our radar.In our view, Foundayo's Spring-ish launch is favorable vs oral Wegovy's Winter launch.
  • No material shifts to switchRx vs last week. Looking back, oral Weg's impact on the switch market wasn't really all that dramatic, maybe some weakness to Zep vial. Even on NBRx, it's hard to say that oral Weg hindered injectables. Whether Foundayo's launch is causing switchRx / NBRx lines to start cross will be important to teasing out competitive and cannibalization effects.
  • All else being equal, it looks like convenience does result in switching. Case in point is Zep's Kwikpen which looks like it may be putting a dent in Zep vial uptake.
  • Also on our radar is BALANCE threshold of 80% being met - 4/30/2026 deadline. We think enough Part D plans will apply and BALANCE goes through. Note 4/30 coincides with LLY's 1Q26 print.
