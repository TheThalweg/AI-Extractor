European Capital Goods: Electrical Equipment

Change of voltage: SU to OW; ABB & LR to EW; RXL to UW

ME shock most like 2022/23 playbook. We favour infrastructure capex (Energy/Grid/AI) exposure, backlogs and pricing power, driving upgrades to SU/ABB. Wary of short cycle/construction exposure, hence downgrades to LR & RXL. SIE stays UW on vulnerable end-market exposure & cash gen.

Capex drivers unlikely to change (and may even be enhanced) with record backlogs offering earnings resilience. Big AI and grid capex plans shouldn't be derailed by the Middle East conflict, and delays based on supply chain challenges will likely result in a return to strong pricing power for equipment providers. If there's a rule of thumb from 2022​/​23, then we should expect energy (e.g. LNG) and power capex to also increase.
