US and Iran agree a fragile ceasefire while negotiations start

The announcement of a two-week ceasefire between the US and Iran and the partial reopening of the Strait of Hormuz marks the first meaningful diplomatic breakthrough since hostilities erupted in late February. Yet, despite this initial relief, the situation remains highly fragile. The ceasefire is time limited, key issues such as Iran’s uranium enrichment remain unresolved, and fresh attacks in neighbouring Gulf states underscore the risk of renewed disruption to energy flows.

Against this backdrop, this Macro Flash Note from the CIO Office presents our latest Middle East Scenario Analysis.
