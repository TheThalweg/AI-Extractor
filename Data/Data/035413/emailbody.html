We estimate revenue and non-IFRS OP growth of 9.8% and 7.1% YoY in 1Q26. Normalized games growth from a high base more than offsets faster marketing and FBS growth, whereas AI investments (including Yuanbao redpockets during CNY) started to weigh on margins. Reiterate OW; PT unchanged at HK$650.
Key takeaways
  • Revenue +9.8%; VAS +6.9% (games +10.0%); marketing +19.0%; FBS +9.5%.
  • GP +10.9% (GPM 56.4%); non-IFRS OP +7.1% (OPM 37.6%); non-IFRS NP +12.1%.
  • We lower our 2026-28 non-IFRS OP estimates by 0.3-0.5%.

VAS revenue up 6.9% YoY, online games up 10.0%: We expect domestic and international games to grow 8.0% and 15.0% YoY, respectively. Growth moderated due to a high base and a later CNY domestically, and fading tailwinds from studio M&A and the Supercell deferral cycle overseas. Sensor Tower show Valorant Mobile hitting a historical high in Feb, while new titles Assault Fire: Future (launched in Jan 26, revenue spike in CNY) and Roco Kingdom: World (No.1 in free-download chart since launched in Mar 26) attribute incremental growth. We expect HoK World (PC launched in Apr 10; mobile later in Apr) to support 2Q26 growth. Social networks slowed, reflecting subscription based businesses (Tencent Video and TME).

Marketing services up 19.0% YoY, improved vs 4Q25: driven by continued adtech benefits, increased ad loads on Video Accounts, and a smaller contribution from e-commerce (which was the main reason of drag in 4Q25). This aligns with management's indication of stronger 1Q26 QTD YoY growth vs 4Q25.

FBS 9.5% YoY (vs. 8.4% in 4Q25): Business services growth could accelerate to 25% YoY (vs. 22% in 4Q25) driven by faster cloud revenue growth amid stronger demand and an improving pricing environment. In addition, commercial payments should benefit from a gradual recovery in consumption during Jan/Feb.

Non-IFRS OP up 7.1% YoY, lagging revenue growth: We forecast non-IFRS operating profit to increase 7.1% YoY, trailing revenue growth, reflecting higher AI investments, in line with guidance to more than double AI opex in 2026. We expect flat to low-single-digit OP growth in 2026 (MSe: non-IFRS OP +4.8% YoY).

OW; PT unchanged at HK$650: We trim 2026-28 revenue forecasts by 0.6-0.8% on lower social network growth and reduce non-IFRS OP estimates by 0.3-0.5%.
