Swiss equities are rising above the fog of war

Local stability in times of global turbulence

 

 

 

 

Swiss equities’ recent underperformance is largely the result of temporary sector effects – particularly the absence of energy exposure and a lagging healthcare sector – rather than of any deterioration in fundamentals. For investors, this creates a favourable set-up: if growth slows or recession risks rise, Switzerland’s defensive characteristics, resilient earnings, and attractive valuations position the market for outperformance. With solid fundamentals, a century‑long record of strong returns, and compelling value today, the current environment offers a timely opportunity to reassess and potentially increase Swiss equity exposure.
