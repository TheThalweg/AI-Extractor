Wages stronger than expected, but BoJ wavers around tradeoff

Total cash earnings and scheduled earnings both rose clearly in February, while negotiated wage hikes remained robust in the third tally, including among SMEs. After a flurry of hawkish signals, BoJ emphasized economic risk at its branch mgrs meeting, wavering around the tradeoff with inflation.
