Americas Payment Technology: 1Q26 Preview: sentiment and valuations find new lows amid resilient (strong?) consumer spending
13 April 2026 | 2:17AM EDT | Research | Equity

After falling by an average of 13% in 2025, the average stock in our coverage has declined by another 22% YTD. We would highlight several debates weighing on sentiment, including 1) AI disruption risk (somewhat vaguely defined, at times) 2) Stablecoins (mostly tied to agentic commerce) and 3) competitive concerns (largely in POS payments, but also in pockets of core banking and consumer fintech). Despite the narrative-driven performance, however, fundamentals have been healthy, if not outright strong, with U.S. volume growth remaining robust, and with March spending inflecting modestly higher on the back of higher gas prices and elevated tax refunds.
